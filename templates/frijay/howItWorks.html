How It Works

A modern way to join a Shabbat-Dinner

Join as a Guest

Step One: Join Frijay

Become a member of this wonderful site and join many Shabbatable dinners around your area

Step Two: Join a Dinner

After becoming a member, use the nav-bar on top of the page to get to the "Join a Dinner" page and reserve a seat for a dinner of your preference. Upon acceptance from the Host of that dinner, you will receive a text-message on your phone with the Host address and contact information. NOTE you can cancel your reservation at anytime by going into the "Dinners I'm Going to" page

Step Three: Enjoy the Dinner

Use the given information of the Host in the confirmation text-message to reach the Dinner place and enjoy a traditional Shabbat Dinner

Host a Dinner

Step One: Join Frijay

Become a member of this wonderful site and login to the site.

Step Two: Host a Dinner

After logging in the nav-bar will give you an option of Hosting a Dinner. In the "Host a Dinner" page provide the information of your event and submit using "Host a Dinner" button. You can view the events you're hosting in the "Dinner I'm Hosting" page.

Step Three: Approve the guests

Now that you are hosting an event, every time a guest reserve a seat in your event you will see the guest name under Pending Reservation section and only upon your approval the guest will be able to join the event. NOTE you can cancel the event at any time
